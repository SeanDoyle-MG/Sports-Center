Adult Volleyball Programs

Men & Women of all ages and ability are invited to participate in Tali Sports Center's adult volleyball programs

Schedules

Season length (including post-season play for All Teams!) by league type include:

Indoor leagues = 8-11 match seasons

FALL (Sep/Oct-Dec)

WINTER (Jan-Mar)

SPRING (Mar-May)

Tali Sports Center offers the following adult volleyball leagues:

REC - Players with low to moderate skill level, the most recreational league we offer. Skill improvement is a benefit & everyone has a great time!
BRONZE - Mix of players with moderate to average skill proficiency & game knowledge. Focus on skill improvement & working on the team concept.
SILVER - Mix of players with above-average to good skill proficiency & good game knowledge. Focus on evenly-matched, similar-skilled grouping of teams.
GOLD - All players have solid, consistent, higher skill proficiency & game knowledge. Focus on quality-match competition.

TSC offers multiple Adult League (age 19+ to be eligible) divisions of COED, MEN'S & WOMEN'S play including 6's (coed=3W/3M) & 4's (coed=2W/2M) on the court at all times. We recommend rosters of 8-15 people per team. League fees (include tax) as well as all facility costs, league equipment, paid officials (for indoor leagues), prizes for winners & more! During post-season play, teams should be prepared to play multiple matches each league day as bracket play dictates. Match times vary each week on your league day. Sundays (or other non-standard league days) may be utilized to make-up matches lost to weather-related issues whenever possible during the league season.
