Golf at Tali Sports Center

Full Swing Golf Simulator. Year-round, rain or shine.

Performance. Realism. Endurance.

Tali Sports Center offers 67 championship courses from around the world in the Full Swing Golf suite.

Our indoor simulator provides the most realistic golfing experience you can have off the course. Ball will fly, bounce, spin and kick back and you can see shots bounce off trees, or splash into the water.

Practice your Golf Swing Anytime

Challenge your friends all year-round. Play realistic championship courses from around the world. Real course conditions. Use your clubs from your first drive, to your last putt.

Rates

Full Swing Golf is $25 per hour and includes unlimited players for the duration. Typically, 18 virtual holes of golf may be played in an hour.

Club Rentals

Tali Sports Center offers golf club rentals. Men's right & left and women's right available for $15 per session. Bring your own clubs and pay no rental charge.
